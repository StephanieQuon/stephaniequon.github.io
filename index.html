The Sprouts Initiative

So far, we've received $67,000 in grants, and involved 300+ individuals in leadership! Our sustainability projects include donating 10,000+ end-of-day meals (2017), distributing 2,250 reusable straws across Canada (2018), collecting 1000+ pledges (2018). We've received recognition through Scotiabank, The Canadian Wildlife Federation, The Globe and Mail, Bloomberg, The Canadian Museum of Nature, NAAEE, and more! I'm super grateful for the opportunity to do this work, and more exciting projects are in the works!
