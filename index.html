The Sprouts Initiative

Founder and President | August 2017 - Present

In August 2017, I founded the Sprouts Initiative! The organization focuses on three pillars: accessibility, sustainability, and community engagament. So far, we've received $67,000 in grants, and involved 300+ individuals in leadership! Our sustainability projects include donating 10,000+ end-of-day meals (2017), distributing 2,250 reusable straws across Canada (2018), collecting 1000+ pledges (2018). Our community engagement projects include distributing 3,000 stickers and 10,000 cards for an interactive art project (2020), donating 500 care packages (2019), our COVID-19 Stories Project (2020), and other COVID-19 relief projects that we received $6000 in grants for (2020). Our accessibility projects include winning $50,000 over 7 grant projects to fund accessible technology with partner organizations across Canada. We've received recognition through Scotiabank, The Canadian Wildlife Federation, The Globe and Mail, Bloomberg, The Canadian Museum of Nature, NAAEE, and more! I'm super grateful for the opportunity to do this work, and more exciting projects are in the works! Please note that it is not ideal that I post information about the initiative here. I do not want to draw attention to myself, but rather the work we've done. However, a website is expensive, and we decided that our funding would be better spent on our core projects.
